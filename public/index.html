How it works
TikTok pulls the video directly from your URL — no file upload needed. The video must be publicly accessible, MP4 / WebM, ≤ 60 min, and ≤ 4 GB. After submitting you'll see a publish_id; the video appears on TikTok once processing is done (usually a few seconds).
